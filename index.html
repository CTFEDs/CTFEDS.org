Cape Town Front-end Developers (CTFEDs) is a free monthly meetup group that hosts talks on Front-end Development and related topics.

Hang around after the talk to chat with friends old and new.

Join us at meetup.com/ctfeds to be notified of our next event.

Call for speakers

  • You do not have to be an experienced speaker. We're happy to help in any way needed, like planning the content of the talk or doing a dry run.
  • We tend to do talks, but if you'd like to do a panel or workshop, let us know!
  • When we have one talk, it's about 40 minutes. We we have two talks, each one is about 20 minutes. Pick the length that you prefer.
  • The topic should mostly be about Front-end development, but can touch on related topics like process and workflow.
  • Read and follow our code of conduct.
