CTFEDs (Cape Town Front-end Developers) is a (mostly) monthly meetup group that hosts talks and workshops on Front-end Development and related topics. The number of people at each event varies, but it tends to hover between 20 and 30.

Our first meetup was in October 2012, and since then we've had {{ site.data.stats.events }} meetups and workshops with over {{ site.data.stats.attendeesish }} atteendes. Our meetup group has about {{ site.data.stats.meetupmembersish }} members, including Front-end Developers, Back-end Developers, Full stack Developers, UX Designers, and more.

We're always looking for venues to hold our meetups and for drinks for our attendees. (The venue needs a projector and to have room for about 30 people. We're usually somewhere in the CBD to avoid the post-work-leaving-town traffic. For drinks we usually supply some beers and ciders and a selection of soft drinks.) Can you help? Let us know! :)
